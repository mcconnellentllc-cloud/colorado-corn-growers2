SDRP Stage 1 Round 2 Payment Expected — Stage 2 Sign-Up Deadline Extended to August 12

Producers with approved Stage 1 SDRP applications will receive an additional 35% top-up payment, doubling the payment factor from 35% to 70%. We're hopeful these payments will begin arriving in early May. Stage 2 applications remain open until the extended August 12, 2026 deadline.
