We Helped Build CLIP - Let Us Help You Sell It

The Colorado Corn Growers Association worked alongside Texas Corn Producers and AgriLogic Consulting to conceive and develop the Crop & Livestock Income Protection (CLIP) program - a groundbreaking new umbrella insurance option for diversified farming operations.

Now we want to help crop insurance agencies understand and offer this valuable coverage to their clients. Our team has the insider knowledge on how CLIP works, why it was created, and how it can benefit your customers.

CLIP provides federally subsidized umbrella revenue protection that works alongside existing Federal Revenue Protection policies - allowing diversified operations to access higher coverage levels at more affordable rates.
