Voluntary Partnership, Measurable Results

The Colorado Corn Growers Association operates through voluntary support, distinguishing us from organizations funded through automatic assessments. We collect no checkoff dollars and receive no mandated fees.

This independence is our strength. It means we can advocate aggressively for what's right for agriculture without worrying about political sensitivities or bureaucratic constraints. Every dollar we receive is a conscious investment in effective advocacy.

Your sponsorship directly funds the lobbying efforts, policy battles, and coalition building that delivers the billion-dollar results our track record demonstrates.
