OUR POWERFUL ALLIANCE

The Growers Association of Colorado Corn is proud to partner with the Southwest Council of Agribusiness (SWCA) - a coalition of grower associations, agriculture enterprises, and financial institutions across Colorado, Kansas, New Mexico, Oklahoma, and Texas.

Through our alliance with SWCA, we leverage the expertise of policy veterans like Tom Sell and former Congressman Larry Combest to fight battles in Washington D.C. that Colorado corn growers could never win alone.

OUR PROVEN TRACK RECORD

Disaster Program Victories

For years, we've fought to ensure Colorado farmers get their fair share of federal disaster assistance. When drought, hail, or other disasters strike, our lobbying efforts have secured millions in emergency aid for Colorado corn growers.

Taking on the Corn Belt

We've challenged the Corn Belt's dominance to ensure Colorado gets equitable opportunities to grow corn. Our advocacy has secured fair treatment in crop insurance, conservation programs, and research funding - ensuring Western agriculture isn't left behind.

Water Rights Protection

Colorado's water rights are under constant attack. We've successfully defended agricultural water rights in court and in legislatures, ensuring corn growers maintain access to the water they need to feed America.

Farm Bill Wins

Every Farm Bill cycle, we fight for provisions that benefit Colorado corn growers. From crop insurance improvements to conservation program enhancements, our advocacy has brought millions in additional support to Colorado agriculture.

Trade and Market Access

Working with our SWCA alliance, we've opened new markets for Colorado corn exports and defended against unfair trade practices that hurt American farmers. Our efforts have secured better prices and expanded opportunities for Colorado corn.

Regulatory Relief

We've successfully fought against burdensome EPA regulations, OSHA overreach, and other federal rules that threatened to crush Colorado farms. Our advocacy has saved Colorado corn growers millions in compliance costs.
