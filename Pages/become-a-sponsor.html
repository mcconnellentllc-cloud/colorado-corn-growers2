RECENT VICTORIES YOUR SPONSORSHIP POWERED

SDRP Game-Changer Victory

Expanded Statewide Coverage

CCGA's advocacy through SWCA partnerships successfully fought to include excessive heat as a qualifying peril in SDRP. This single victory expanded Colorado's eligibility from just 24 drought-designated counties to statewide coverage - potentially multiplying Colorado's share of the $16 billion SDRP program.

Impact: Without this advocacy win, most of Colorado would have been left out of critical disaster payments.

Market Facilitation Programs

$100+ Million

When trade wars threatened Colorado corn markets, CCGA's Washington advocacy secured over $100 million in Market Facilitation Program payments for Colorado corn growers. Our board's direct connections to policymakers ensured Colorado wasn't left behind.

COVID-19 Relief Programs

$400+ Million

Through CFAP 1 and CFAP 2, CCGA's persistent advocacy secured over $400 million in critical COVID-19 relief for Colorado agriculture. Our Friday morning Washington calls ensured Colorado got its fair share when farmers needed it most.

Crop Insurance & Conservation

$500+ Million

CCGA's advocacy has delivered over $300 million in ARC and PLC payments, over $200 million in PPP loans, and over $500 million in crop insurance indemnities. Our board's expertise in insurance law and federal programs makes the difference.

Emergency Commodity Assistance

$8 Billion Nationally

CCGA's advocacy through SWCA partnerships helped secure ECAP (Emergency Commodity Assistance Program) which delivered $8 billion nationally for 2024 economic losses. Colorado corn growers benefited directly from this national victory.

Regulatory Defense Victories

Millions Saved

When SB-021 proposed unjust labor regulations that would have devastated family farms, CCGA's political connections and advocacy expertise successfully defended Colorado agriculture, saving corn growers millions in compliance costs.
