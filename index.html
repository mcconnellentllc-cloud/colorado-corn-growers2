About Colorado Corn Growers Association

Our Mission

We are a membership-based organization that works tirelessly to ensure that the voice of Colorado's corn producers is heard loud and clear, both in our state capitol and in Washington, D.C.

We are not the Colorado Corn Promotion Council - we are the grassroots advocacy organization that fights for policies that benefit our corn growers. Affiliated with the Southwest Council of Agribusiness and working alongside Tom Sell, we bring real lobbying power to agricultural issues that matter most to our members.

Our Legislative Accomplishments

  • Championed the Renewable Fuel Standard (RFS) protecting ethanol markets for corn growers
  • Advocated for Farm Bill provisions ensuring crop insurance and risk management tools
  • Fought against EPA proposals that would reduce ethanol blending requirements
  • Secured water rights protections for Colorado agricultural producers
  • Lobbied for agricultural worker legislation that balances farmer needs with labor rights
  • Maintained active PAC contributing to pro-agriculture candidates at state and federal levels
  • Protected corn growers from burdensome environmental regulations through strategic advocacy

Meet Our Board

Leadership Team

Our board consists of dedicated Colorado corn producers who volunteer their time to ensure our association represents the best interests of all Colorado corn growers.

These farmers bring decades of experience in corn production, agricultural business, and policy advocacy. They work closely with our lobbying partners to identify key issues and develop strategic approaches to protect and advance the interests of Colorado corn growers.

Board information and member profiles will be updated here. Contact our office for current board member information and meeting schedules.
